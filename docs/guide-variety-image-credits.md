# Guide Variety Image Credits

PALAYSCAN uses real rice reference photos for the rice variety guide visuals. These are not exact NSIC variety photos. They are used as rice/palay reference visuals while the variety-specific information is shown in the readable text and fact cards.

The 15 local variety images in `assets/guide/varieties/` were cropped and lightly color-washed from these reusable Wikimedia Commons files:

- `Rice plant with grains.jpg` by Damien Boilley / Damouns, licensed under CC BY 2.0.
  Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Rice_plant_with_grains.jpg
- `Rice panicle.jpg` by Indrakriya, licensed under CC BY-SA 4.0.
  Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Rice_panicle.jpg
- `Rice Panicle.jpg` by Ramesh Kunnappully, licensed under CC BY-SA 4.0.
  Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Rice_Panicle.jpg

If the team later captures its own field photos or gets authorized PhilRice/PinoyRice variety photos, replace these reference visuals with those exact photos and update this file.
